About Me

I'm an Assistant Professor in the Space, Planetary, Astronomical Sciences and Engineering (SPASE) Department at IIT Kanpur. Before this, I was a Senior Research Engineer at the Onsala Space Observatory -- The Swedish national Infrastructure for Radio Astronomy -- operated by the Chalmers University. My interests are in Radio Astronomy, Radio Interferometry and developing antennas, analog and digital instrumentation for radio astronomy. I'm interested in Solar and Non-solar transients, and more closer to earth, the ionosphere and natural radio emissions. We are also starting to work on using signals around us to passively study moving objects :D I hold a PhD in astronomical instrumentation from the Indian Institute of Astrophysics.

Research

  • Digital Receivers for Transients and VLBI Observations
  • Metrology of big reflectors
  • Space weather observations using Pulsars
  • Developing radiometers to probe the atmosphere
  • Detecting signatures of cosmic rays in radio wavelengths.

Students interested in pursuing projects in Antenna Design, Microwave systems, Quasi-optics, Digital Receivers, Passive Radars and data analysis can contact me at mugundhanv@iitk.ac.in.
